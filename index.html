PRINCIPAL'S DESK

"A true teacher would never tell you what to do. But he woud give you the knowledge with which you could decide what would be the best for you to do."

-Christopher Pike, sati

It is with great pride and joy that I welcome you to D.D. Public School, a place where dreams take root and futures are shaped. As your Principal, my commitment is to foster an environment of academic excellence, creativity, and holistic growth. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to inspire every student to reach their fullest potential, blending traditional values with modern learning techniques.

This year, we aim to strengthen our focus on innovation, inclusivity, and community service. I encourage each of you to participate actively in school activities, embrace challenges, and uphold the values of respect and integrity. Together, let’s build a vibrant learning community that prepares our students to thrive in a dynamic world.

Thank you for your unwavering support and trust. I look forward to an enriching academic session filled with achievements and memorable moments.

ABOUT CAMPUS


Established in 2009 by Mr. Chanrda Prakesh Patel and Mr. Brijesh Verma, D.D. Public School Goshainganj AmbedkarNagar Campus fosters an environment for self-learning. Our Founder's visionary guidance supports our continuous growth.

Led by Mr. Chandra Prakesh Patel and Principal Mr. Brijesh Verma, we make learning participative and enjoyable, focusing on academic excellence and holistic development. Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals through rigorous academics and diverse extracurricular activities.

As part of the globally recognized D.D. family, we blend academic rigor with strong moral values. Our highly qualified teaching staff fosters a love for learning and critical thinking. We emphasize community service and global citizenship, encouraging students to engage in social initiatives and international programs. Our inclusive atmosphere ensures personalized attention, helping every child realize their potential and emerge as confident, compassionate leaders committed to humanity’s service.
